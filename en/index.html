welcome!

A Canadian school in the Serra Gaúcha.

If your family is moving to — or already living in — Bento Gonçalves, the capital of Brazilian wine in the Serra Gaúcha highlands of southern Brazil, you have exactly one option for a Canadian education: us. Maple Bear Bento Gonçalves is the only Canadian school in the city, operating since 2025 just minutes from the Vale dos Vinhedos, the country's most celebrated wine region.

We are part of Maple Bear Global Schools, a network of more than 600 schools in over 30 countries built on the methodology of the Canadian education system — consistently ranked among the world's best in PISA and OECD assessments. Wherever your family goes next, a Maple Bear education travels with your child.

True English immersion, fully accredited in Brazil.

At Maple Bear, English is not a subject — it is the language of the classroom. Children spend their day immersed in English through Canadian-trained teachers, play-based inquiry, songs, stories and projects, while also developing full literacy in Portuguese. The result is functional bilingualism: children who think, play and make friends in two languages.

At the same time, our program is fully aligned with the BNCC, Brazil's national curriculum framework. That means your child receives an internationally recognized Canadian methodology and a complete, accredited Brazilian education — no gaps, no catch-up needed if your family relocates within Brazil or returns home.

Programs by age.

Bear Care

18 months

A gentle first school experience. Warm, predictable routines and soft bilingual immersion through music, stories and sensory play.

Toddler

2 years

Language explodes — in two languages at once, without translation. Growing autonomy and symbolic play as the main learning tool.

Nursery

3 years

Letters, numbers and colours in English and Portuguese. Early literacy, inquiry projects guided by children's interests, structured socialization.

Junior Kindergarten (JK)

4 years

Pre-literacy in both languages, early mathematical thinking, group work and active listening.

Senior Kindergarten (SK)

5 years

Emerging independent reading in English and Portuguese, hands-on math, integrated social-emotional learning — full preparation for elementary school.

Year 1 — Elementary

6 – 7 years · opening in 2027

The first year of elementary school, in the same place, in two languages. Canadian curriculum aligned with the BNCC, full bilingual literacy.

Why international families choose us.

  • Continuity: a global network means your child can transition between Maple Bear schools across 30+ countries.
  • English-speaking staff: tours, admissions and day-to-day communication are available in English.
  • Soft landing: for children arriving from abroad, the bilingual classroom eases the move into Brazilian life while keeping English alive every day.
  • Quality of life: Bento Gonçalves combines the safety and charm of a mid-sized wine-country town with strong schools, healthcare and an exceptional food and wine scene.
  • Canadian methodology: inquiry-based learning, continuous qualitative assessment, and integrated social-emotional education.
Many of our families work in the wine, furniture and technology industries that make this region one of southern Brazil's most dynamic. Some are returning Brazilians; others are arriving for the first time. All of them find a school that speaks their language — literally.
